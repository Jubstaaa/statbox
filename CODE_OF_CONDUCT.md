# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the product and code.
- Respect boundaries and different experience levels.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Repeated bad-faith arguments or spam
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors
who violate these standards.

## Reporting

If needed, contact the maintainer through the public repository or linked
maintainer website.
